About Shimal environmental management Services

Shimal Agro-Tech & environmental services is private company registered as an agro-technology and environmental services organization.
Our services include routine garden care, lanscaping, eco-friendly fumigation and refuse disposal.

What Behind Shimal environmental management Services

We aim to provide residents, corporate organizations, factories, schools, restaurants and the community with access to healthy environmental services at an affordable rate.

Shimal Agro-Tech & environmental services is an "environment-centric" organization with aim of contributing to the existing projects initiated by the United Nations under the sustainable Development Goals. Data generation is vital aspect of our movement; the information helps Support research toward improved understanding, modeling, and prediction of the environmental system.
